Welcome. This is Ajaycent Angles — a blog about tech, finance, politics, and philosophy, and more importantly about the places where they touch.

The name is a play on "adjacent angles": two angles that share a common side. That's the editorial thesis here. Most interesting ideas live at the boundary between two fields — where a concept from game design explains something about markets, or a philosophical framework clarifies a policy debate. I'd rather explore those shared edges than stay safely inside one lane.

## What's here now

The first project on this site is the [2026 Election Tracker](https://elections.ajaycent.com) — a live dashboard tracking Senate and House races through the 2026 midterms, with polling trends, race ratings, and news for every contest. Posts analyzing what the data shows will land here on the blog.

## What's coming

Essays and analysis, published when I have something worth saying rather than on a schedule. Expect the four core topics above, plus the occasional wildcard — sometimes the most instructive angle on one hobby comes from a completely different one.

Thanks for reading.
